Notice of Privacy Practices

Allcare Acupuncture & Herb Clinic

THIS NOTICE DESCRIBES HOW MEDICAL INFORMATION ABOUT YOU MAY BE USED AND DISCLOSED AND HOW YOU CAN GET ACCESS TO THIS INFORMATION. PLEASE REVIEW IT CAREFULLY.

Introduction

This Notice describes how ALLCARE Acupuncture and Herb CLINIC may use or disclose your protected health information (PHI). "We" and "I" means ALLCARE Acupuncture and Herb CLINIC. "You" and "your" means our patient.

Use and Disclosure of Protected Health Information

ALLCARE Acupuncture and Herb CLINIC may use and disclose your PHI for each of the following purposes:

1. Treatment

We may use your PHI to provide, coordinate or manage health care and related services by one or more health care providers. For example:

  • Sharing information with other healthcare providers who are treating you
  • Consulting with another provider about your care
  • Referring you to another healthcare provider

2. Payment

We may use and disclose your health information to obtain payment from health plans and insurers for the care that we provide to you. This may include:

  • Submitting claims to your health insurance company
  • Verifying your insurance coverage
  • Obtaining prior authorization for treatment

3. Healthcare Operations

Include the business aspects of running our practice, such as:

  • Conducting quality assessment and improvement activities
  • Employee review activities
  • Licensing and credentialing
  • Business planning and development
  • General administrative activities

Your Rights Regarding Your Health Information

You have the following rights with respect to your PHI:

Request Restrictions

You can request a restriction on certain uses and disclosures of your PHI. We are not required to agree to all restrictions, but must agree to certain requests regarding disclosures to health plans.

Inspect and Copy

You have the right to inspect and receive a copy of your PHI that we maintain in designated record sets.

Amend

You have the right to request that we amend your PHI if you believe it is incorrect or incomplete.

Accounting of Disclosures

You have the right to receive an accounting of certain disclosures we have made of your PHI.

Our Responsibilities

ALLCARE Acupuncture and Herb CLINIC is required by law to:

  • Maintain the privacy of your protected health information
  • Provide individuals with notice of our legal duties and privacy practices with respect to PHI
  • Notify you following a breach of unsecured PHI
  • Abide by the terms of this notice

Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to change this notice and make the new notice apply to PHI we already have as well as any information we receive in the future. We will post a copy of our current notice at our office. The notice will contain the effective date on the first page.

Questions or Complaints

If you have questions about this notice or believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may contact:

ALLCARE Acupuncture and Herb CLINIC

Privacy Officer

Email: info@aaahcc.com

You can also file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights. We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint.
